IAM Onboarding Overhaul

Cutting B2B identity provider setup from 4 days to a few hours, by redesigning the mental model, not just the interface.

Client
miniOrange
Year
2023 to 2026
Role
Sr. Product Designer
Domain
Identity & Access Management

The problem

Enterprise customers setting up Single Sign-On and identity federation were taking 4+ days from contract signature to working integration. The setup process required deep technical knowledge, had no progressive disclosure, and failed silently at multiple points.

Root cause analysis

Root cause 01

Wrong mental model

The UI was organised around internal system concepts, not how IT admins think about identity federation. Customers had to translate constantly.

"I know what SAML is. I don't know what 'Service Provider Entity ID' means in your system."
Root cause 02

No progressive disclosure

All 23 configuration options were presented at once, regardless of the customer's IdP, use case, or technical maturity.

"Why am I being asked about token expiry before I've even connected my directory?"
Root cause 03

Silent failures

Integration tests either passed or showed a raw error code. No guidance on what went wrong, why, or how to fix it.

"Error 403. Cool. No idea what that means in this context."
Root cause 04

No defaults

Every field required a decision. Secure, sensible defaults for common configurations would have eliminated 60% of the configuration burden.

"Just tell me what most companies like mine choose. I'll change it if I need to."

Design approach

01
IdP-first flow
Onboarding now starts by asking which identity provider the customer uses. Subsequent steps are tailored: Okta customers see Okta-specific instructions, field labels, and screenshots.
02
Progressive disclosure
Collapsed the 23-step flow into a 5-step wizard. Advanced options are accessible but hidden behind "Customise", sensible defaults pre-filled throughout.
03
Contextual error recovery
Every failure state now includes: what went wrong, why it likely happened, and the exact steps to fix it, specific to the customer's IdP and configuration.
04
Live integration testing
An embedded test-as-you-go panel lets admins verify each step before proceeding. Failures are caught early, not after completing the full 23-step flow.
