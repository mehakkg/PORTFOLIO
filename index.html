How I work

Three ways to bring me into your team.

Every engagement is scoped to what the product actually needs — not a packaged offering retrofitted to your problem.

E · 01

Embedded design slice

I join your team for a defined sprint or quarter. Strategy, IA, execution — from your Figma file, in your Slack, shipping with your engineers.

Best for: teams moving fast without a senior designer
E · 02

Enterprise UX Audit

A scenario-driven audit of your product. Named problems, prioritised opportunities, a roadmap you can defend to leadership.

Best for: products stuck between versions
E · 03

Design systems & scale

Foundations that let your team move faster. Tokens, components, usage rules — built for your stack and documented for the next designer.

Best for: multi-team orgs with a consistency problem
Point of view

What I believe about good design.

Convictions I've earned by being wrong first. These shape every engagement.

B · 01

Strategy before screens.

By the time I open Figma, the architecture is decided. Pixels are downstream of decisions.

B · 02

Audit before redesign.

You can't fix what you haven't named. A scenario-driven audit beats a moodboard every time.

B · 03

Adoption over aesthetic.

A product no one opens is a portfolio piece, not a product. Solve adoption first, beauty second.

B · 04

Compliance is a design surface.

SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO — these aren't engineering checklists. They shape what users can do, when, and how.
